About Me

Simbarashe Joworingo

Hi there! I am a 3rd-year Mechanical Engineering student at Aditya College of Engineering & Technology under JNTUK, Andhra Pradesh, India. My engineering journey started with a fascination for how machines work — taking things apart and building better versions.

That curiosity has evolved into a focused skillset in CAD modelling across SolidWorks, CATIA, and Siemens NX, Python-based engineering simulation, and computational fluid dynamics. I maintain a CGPA of 8.84/10.

Active member of the IMEC Robotics Club. Completed hands-on workshops in UAV assembly (EF16 Hexcopter — Mechanical Designer role) and Electric Vehicle design. Currently preparing for internship roles in applied mechanics and aerospace systems.
