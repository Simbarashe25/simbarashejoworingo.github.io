CAD · Systems Design
UAV System Design & Integration
Designed and integrated a fixed-wing UAV system — from SolidWorks fuselage and wing modelling to aerodynamic trade-off analysis and internal avionics wiring layout.

What I Did

  • Designed system-level CAD models for a fixed-wing UAV fuselage and wings in SolidWorks, optimised for specific mission payload and range requirements
  • Executed design segmentation for additive manufacturing to reduce part count while maintaining structural integrity and assembly alignment
  • Planned complex internal wiring routes for avionics and control surfaces, minimising electromagnetic interference and service access time
  • Performed conceptual aircraft sizing and aerodynamic trade-off analysis — wing loading, aspect ratio, lift-drag behaviour — for low-Reynolds-number flight
  • Selected a high-lift airfoil for short takeoff by analysing trade-offs between aspect ratio and structural stiffness

What I Learned

  • Translating mission requirements into geometric and structural constraints is harder than it looks — everything is a trade-off
  • Designing for additive manufacturing requires thinking about assembly before the first sketch line
  • Low-Reynolds-number aerodynamics behaves very differently from textbook high-Re assumptions

Python · Thermodynamics
Supercharger Effects on IC Engine Performance
Used Python to model and visualise how supercharging affects IC engine performance — analysing boost pressure, thermodynamic parameters, and generating characteristic performance curves.

What I Did

  • Developed Python scripts to analyse and visualise supercharger impact on engine performance, creating performance curves for various boost pressures
  • Modelled thermodynamic parameters: air-fuel ratio, combustion efficiency, volumetric efficiency — achieving a simulated 134% volumetric efficiency
  • Analysed the influence of intake pressure and temperature rise on engine breathing characteristics
  • Generated boost pressure vs power and efficiency vs speed curves using Matplotlib and Pandas

What I Learned

  • Supercharging doesn't just add power — it fundamentally changes thermal behaviour and introduces new design constraints
  • Data visualisation is not decoration — it's the main tool for engineering insight
  • Writing the model in code forces you to understand the equations before you can even run it
Python Matplotlib Pandas Thermodynamics IC Engine Theory
