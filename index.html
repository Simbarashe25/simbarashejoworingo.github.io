About Me

Simbarashe Joworingo

Hi there! I'm a 3rd-year Mechanical Engineering student at Aditya College of Engineering & Technology under JNTUK, Andhra Pradesh, India. My engineering journey started with a fascination for how machines work — taking things apart, understanding them, and building better versions.

That curiosity has evolved into a focused skillset in CAD modelling across SolidWorks, CATIA, and Siemens NX, Python-based engineering simulation, and computational fluid dynamics. I maintain a CGPA of 8.84/10 and actively apply my coursework to real engineering problems.

I'm an active member of the IMEC Robotics Club and have completed hands-on workshops in UAV assembly and Electric Vehicle design. Currently preparing for engineering internship roles in applied mechanics, aerospace systems, and numerical simulation.
